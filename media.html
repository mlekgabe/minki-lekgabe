Professional Interests

My Presentation

My professional interests encompass collaborative curriculum design, educational research, and the development of teacher capacity, all underscored by a strong dedication to enhancing teaching and learning outcomes. I am particularly interested in research that focuses on inclusive and sustainable educational transformation. I am currently a curriculum developer based at the Department of Curriculum Development & Evaluation, Ministry of Child Welfare and Basic Education in Botswana.
I also coordinate the Learning Support Materials office for the ministry. I have contributed to the reconfiguration of the General Education Curriculum and Assessment Framework (GECAF) to make it Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, Mathematics (STEAM) oriented as the GECAF Committee Secretary. I also contributed to developing the Botswana Languages in Policy in Education. I am currently honing my research communication with the renowned Leaders of Africa Research Institute.
